## 2026-04-16 - Add ARIA Labels to Icon-Only Buttons in Reference Components
**Learning:** Sighted users can easily identify icon-only buttons via visual cues, but screen readers require descriptive `aria-label` attributes to interpret their function. Without these labels, screen reader users might hear 'button' or unclear content.
**Action:** Add descriptive `aria-label`s to all icon-only buttons (`Copy`, `X`, `Plus`, etc.) ensuring parity between visual and auditory experiences. Specifically implemented in `src/components/ui/reference-export.tsx` and `src/components/ui/reference-form.tsx`.
